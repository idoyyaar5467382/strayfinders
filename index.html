    About the Project

    StrayFinder is a web-based platform dedicated to helping people locate, report, and care for stray cats in urban and suburban environments. Our mission is to create a community-driven space where every user can contribute to improving animal welfare by tracking sightings and helping shelters respond faster.

    MEET (Middle East Entrepreneurs of Tomorrow) is a groundbreaking initiative supported by MIT.
