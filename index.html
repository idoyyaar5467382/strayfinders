    About the Project

    StrayFinder is a web-based platform dedicated to helping people locate, report, and care for stray cats within urban and suburban environments. Our mission is to create a community-driven space where every user can contribute to making a real difference in animal welfare by tracking sightings, sharing information, and helping shelters respond faster.
